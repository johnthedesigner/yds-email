How to get the most out of your business membership.

1. Send us your logo and a link to your website

We’re proud to list our business members on our website so send us your logo (high resolution) and website soon and we can start telling everyone about you.

See All of Our Business Members

2. Let people know you’re a proud Y.D.S. member by displaying our badge on your website

Check out our logos & badges usage page to grab a badge image and check out how to share your new Y.D.S. membership!

How to download & use your member badge

3. Get involved and support YDS

The success and growth of Y.D.S is through the support of our members and we are grateful for the various ways our members can get involved. Please reach out if you would like to sponsor a prize in our annual raffle, host a club meeting, be one of our feature speakers or be one of our growing partners.

Get in touch with us

4. Come to our next meeting! It’s the best way to meet people.

Come join the fun, add your energy and meet dahlia growers during our full calendar of fun virtual and in-person meetings.

Meetings & Events

5. Tell others about Y.D.S.

Whether on social media or in person, Y.D.S. appreciates sharing with dahlia growers both new or experienced about our club. The strength in our club is the wonderful community we build around it. Our club has so much to offer and so much membership value, it’s a resource that shouldn’t be a secret.

We appreciate being tagged by our members in Y.D.S. or dahlia-related posts (@yankeedahliasociety on Instagram and Facebook) and we encourage using the hashtag #yankeedahliasociety for online posts and conversation that are relevant to us and our members.

6. Do you sell tubers?

We love buying tubers that are grown by our business partners. Please let us know when you will be selling tubers and what varieties you will have available.

Get in touch about selling tubers
