January 2022 Dahlia Tuber Sale

View in browser

Hello dahlia friends!

2021 has been an amazing year of firsts! We are so very excited to be able to hold our first club tuber sale within the first year of forming Yankee Dahlia Society. This sale has been over a year in the making and we have so many people to thank for the success and enthusiasm over the past 12 months. Our fabulous growing partners, the members who’ve donated tubers, members who have volunteered their time and our support team of family members who have lent a hand and donated their expertise to make this happen.

Tuber sales are far and away the biggest and most important source of funding for any club and especially for a new club like ours. We trust that you have enjoyed the programming and new friendships you’ve made and we look forward to an exciting second year ahead. We are so grateful for your commitment to the club and for wanting to purchase your dahlias from Yankee Dahlia Society. Thank you for your support!

The club has grown a large selection of dahlia varieties in all the forms and most sizes for the garden, exhibition and cut flowers. All tubers have been grown in New England by Yankee Dahlia Society Members and Y.D.S. Growing Partners. By buying tubers from the club sale you are supporting our local dahlia community. During the season we are always on the lookout to score desirable varieties and new introductions to increase our stock so we can offer our members an outstanding selection of club tubers. We hope you will find many here that you will want for your own garden.

We are proud to hold our inaugural sale online. We have worked hard, with many dedicated hours behind the scenes, to bring you a beautiful online store and an easy shopping experience. Here are the details for our sale.

– Misty & Carol

Tuber Sale Details

Early Access

Y.D.S. Members who have donated 10 tubers or have volunteered 6 hours of their time towards cutting back, digging and dividing over the past 12 weeks will have exclusive access to our one day club sale on Saturday, January 8, 2022. Opening at 3:00pm through Sunday, January 9, at 3:00pm.

All Member Access

All Y.D.S. Members will have access to our one day club sale on Saturday, January 15, 2022. Opening at 3:00pm through Sunday, January 16th at 3:00pm.

Password

Access to the sale is for Y.D.S. members only. You will need to use the email address you provided at registration and a password provided by Y.D.S. Please look for the email later today following this one with your password to login to the link to the shopping portal on the website.

Tuber Availability

Tubers added to your cart are not confirmed until paid for. Keep in mind that if there is low availability on a tuber you may want to check out immediately to secure the variety. It is possible that a tuber you put in your cart won’t be available upon checkout if there is limited stock.

Tuber Inventory

We will be dividing tubers throughout the week to replenish tuber stock again for our All Y.D.S. Member sale on Saturday, January 15th. We still have more inventory to divide and will be able to offer another sale in early spring. It may include new varieties we haven’t divided and more stock in tubers we sell out of during our January sales.

Inspection Upon Receipt

All our tubers are planted with a label during the growing season to make sure what you are ordering is true to the variety name. We have gone through rigorous quality checks to ensure that all tubers sold have an eye and each tuber has also been labeled with the variety name. Please store your tubers upon receipt.

Pick Up Only

We are offering pick up only for our January sale. Pick up will be at the club New Year Party on Sunday, January 23 at Spence Farm from 11:00am-2:00pm. Having your tubers in hand in January will mean those who might want to propagate stock from the tubers this year will be able to start their tubers early. *For those who may not be able to attend the January 23rd party and meeting, feel free to email info@yankeedahliasociety to arrange for pick up with Misty and Carol.
