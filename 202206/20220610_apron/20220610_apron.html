Hello dahlia friends!

Many of you have asked about our garden aprons. Carol and I have connected with Tammy, a dahlia grower from Greater Philadelphia Dahlia Society who loves to sew! She hand makes these fantastic garden aprons that Carol and I picked up last year. We love having extra pockets when working in the garden and supporting a small business and fellow dahlia grower. They are so helpful that we wear our aprons all the time. They are extremely durable and when they get dirty they wash really well. We love the adjustable belt loop and it’s easy to snap on and off.

We have connected with Tammy and she is happy to make aprons for anyone who would like to order one. She even has a kids apron!

Photo by: Misty Florez

YDS will place the order for all aprons on June 27th. As orders come in we will forward them to Tammy so she can start sewing. Once we receive the finished aprons, we will coordinate pick up, meet up or ship* them to you.

*if requested: shipping fee to you will be invoiced separately
