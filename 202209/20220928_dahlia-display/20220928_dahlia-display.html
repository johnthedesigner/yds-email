October Dahlia Display

View in browser

Hello dahlia friends!

Calling all members to participate this weekend! Come and join in the fun and bring your dahlia blooms to the YDS Dahlia Bloom Exhibit at Westwood Public Library. This will be our first club public dahlia display and we want members (and your dahlia growing friends) to bring lots of your blooms on Saturday morning (10am - 12noon) to share our love for growing dahlias. Let’s end the season with a celebration of The Dahlia with hundreds of blooms to pack the library and excite the public!

Floral Design arrangements are welcome and encouraged! We know that many of you love to create beautiful arrangements, and we would love for you to share your talent.

The exhibit will be hosted in the Community Room in Westwood Public Library (Main Branch, 660 High St) on Saturday and Sunday. Bring your friends and family and come out to enjoy the exhibit and attend the free presentations. We are excited about our first club dahlia display and want all of our members and guests who can attend to participate and have a wonderful time.

– Misty & Carol


Schedule

October 1, 2022, 10:00am*-5:00pm

10:00 am – Staging Blooms & SetUp - Display your dahlias (open to all)

12:00 pm – Dahlia Exhibit Open for Viewing

3:00 pm – Presentation: Beautiful Bouquets with Dahlias from your Garden by YDS Member Chau Ho


October 2, 2022, 2:00pm-5:00pm

2:00 pm – Dahlia Exhibit Open for Viewing

3:00 pm – Presentation: The Joy of Growing Dahlias in New England by Co-Founders & Co-Presidents of Yankee Dahlia Society Carol Palmer & Misty Florez

Details

RSVP on SignUp Genius: RSVP isn’t necessary but we would love to know you’re coming with blooms so we can be ready and we are looking for volunteers to help.

https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10C0C44AFA82DA5FAC16-ydsdahlia

Bring: Individual Dahlia blooms & Floral Arrangements. YDS will supply the display vases for individual blooms.

Staging Blooms: Bring your dahlia blooms & Floral Arrangements on Saturday, October 1st between 10am-12noon. YDS will supply the display vases for individual blooms. We can coordinate with individuals who bring floral arrangements to retrieve your containers after the event.

Volunteers: We will be in need of club members to volunteer Saturday and Sunday with a variety of tasks and Monday to help breakdown. If you have time to help and sign up that would be greatly appreciated. Please see the volunteer opportunities. Thank you so much for chipping in to help.

Guests: This event is open to the public. Please invite your friends and family to join us. If you have dahlia friends that would like to bring blooms please encourage them to do so. The more the merrier!

Address: Westwood Public Library (Community Room), 660 High Street, Westwood, MA 02090

RSVP: Not required, but it would be wonderful to know if you are able to join us as we want to be prepared.
