Dear YDS Growing Partner,

As the 2022 dahlia season peak bloom period is coming to an end, we would like to thank you for your participation as a Growing Partner with YDS this year. We hope you have been enjoying the varieties we selected for you to grow at your farm and that you have found many favorites among them, both old and new.

As the frost is upon us we are getting ready for the work ahead of us and in order to get all the work done in a timely manner we have created a digging schedule. We appreciate your willingness to accommodate club plans and a schedule to help us get it all done. We plan to have every tuber out of the ground by November 20th so that we can enjoy the Thanksgiving week and make preparations for the YDS Great Divide with club members on Sunday, December 4th .

We could use the help of as many YDS club members as possible including your help with the Great Divide. So we hope you can save the date and join us! We are looking to make this a big club event at Spence Farm in Woburn and would appreciate as much help as we can have to divide all the club tubers.

As we dig up tubers and plan to divide, we will be reviewing our Growing Partner Program and resources, and making plans for the 2023 growing season. We appreciate that you too may be considering plans for your 2023 growing season and your availability to partner again with YDS. We welcome you to share your feedback and plans for 2023. As we enter our third YDS growing season in 2023 we may be making some adjustments as we try to find a balance between YDS activities and resources.

We will be in touch to work with each of you separately as we make arrangements and coordinate schedules. We’d love to hear from each of you with ideas, feedback and even tentative plans by December 1 to get the conversations going and give everybody time to make plans. We know it’s been a challenging season. We’d love to see any photos you may have of YDS blooms or plants in your garden if you have any you could share with us. Thank you for being a partner and for all your support.

– Misty & Carol
