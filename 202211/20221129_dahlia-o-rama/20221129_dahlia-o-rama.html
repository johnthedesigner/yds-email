YDS December Dahlia-o-Rama

View in browser

Sunday, December 4, 2022
7:00am to 7:00pm

New this year, YDS will be holding a Dahlia-O-Rama day (day long club tuber dividing session) at Spence Farm in Woburn. It's a big collective effort to get club tubers divided and inventoried for our tuber sale! The sooner we can get all the tubers divided, the sooner we can hold our sale, and we know all of you have been waiting for it. We’re excited and can’t wait for it too!

The YDS Dahlia-O-Rama Day will be held on Sunday, December 4th from 7AM to 7 PM . We’d love for as many of you as can make it to come out that day and help. Spend a few hours or the whole day, really any amount of time you can volunteer will be greatly appreciated. For those who may be new to dividing dahlias, come for one of our two dividing lessons and we will show you the ropes. There’s something for everyone to help with and plenty of tasks that need to be done that don’t require any previous experience, such as labeling, sorting, counting and packing. When YDS members get together it’s always a lot of fun!

We’re looking for experienced dividers to be team leaders for each table group. Expect us to reach out and email members asking you to fill a particular role for the day.

We will also need dedicated help with:

  • Set Up - Loading and unloading tubers and supplies (45 mins early)
  • Breakdown - It’s all got to be packed up afterwards
  • Greeter & Volunteer Placement - Welcome Members & Place Volunteers & Track Volunteer Hours
  • Hospitality Table - Will you make coffee and staff the food table?
  • Dividing Station Leaders - People who are confident in dividing and looking for eyes
  • Write on Tubers - People with legible & neat handwriting
  • Inventory & Sorters - People who like organization, count, check and double check & quality control

Earn Early Access to YDS Tuber Sale

Just like last year, members who give at least six hours of volunteer time at the end of the growing season (lifting, washing, preparing, dividing club tubers) will earn Early Access for a full week to our online club tuber sale before we open it up to all members. This is a valuable club perk, and helping out on December 4th is a great way to get your volunteer hours in for Early Access. **This may be the last opportunity for members to earn Early Access for the tuber sale.

We are excited to have so many new and exciting varieties of dahlias to offer our members in our upcoming tuber sale. It promises to be even better than last year!

Location: Spence Farm @ Hurld Wyman Elementary School, 41 Wyman Street, Woburn, MA 01801

Date & Time: Sunday, December 4, 2022 from 7:00am to 7:00pm (come for a few hours, stay for the whole time or however long you can that day)

Food: You are welcome to bring a box lunch, or you can purchase a 2-slice pizza ticket for $6.00. RSVP for a Pizza ticket through our SignUp Genius. Payment will be taken in advance via Venmo or at the door. Lunch will be at 1:00pm.

Want to bring a small dish or something to share? (Some suggested items to help fuel us for the day)

  • Morning: Muffins, Quiche, Fruit: Bananas, Apples
  • Lunch: Salad
  • Snacks: Cookies, Cheese & Crackers, Veggies & Dip

Remember: To bring your favorite dividing tools (In case you need a new pair or forget yours we will have snips available for purchase.)

Optional: Bring a box lunch or purchase a ticket for 2 pizza slices $6.00. Pay ahead via Venmo (@yankeedahliasociety) or pay at the door.

RSVP through SignUp Genius: An RSVP isn’t required but extremely helpful for us as we make plans. Please let us know you’re coming and what time you can come and if you would like pizza. https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10C0C44AFA82DA5FAC16-20223
