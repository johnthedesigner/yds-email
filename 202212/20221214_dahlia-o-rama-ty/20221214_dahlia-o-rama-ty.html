Thanks for helping with the Dahlia-O-Rama

View in browser

Hello dahlia friends!

What a fantastic event we had at our first Dalia-O-Rama! We want to send out a huge heartfelt thank you to all of you who were able to join us. We had so many members coming throughout the day supporting YDS by pitching in and helping out. Starting from 7 AM on, our club volunteers were ready to work. From dividing, labeling, and sorting to counting, packing and stacking boxes, and bringing lots of food, YDS members came through! We had a big job to be done and we couldn't have done it without your help! Thank you to everyone as the day was a successful event and so much fun!

Photos by: John Livornese

While we got a huge part of our tubers divided for our upcoming tuber sale, there is still some remaining work to be done. We will have a few more opportunities for anyone who would like to pitch in and help get the remainder divided, labeled and counted. If you need to get a few more hours in for Early Access, love to divide or need practice with dividing or finding eyes, or simply have time and want to help out we will have room for a small group of volunteers during the next two weeks.

Check out the SignUp Genius for dates, times and to join us! We appreciate all the help. Thank you so much!

– Misty & Carol
