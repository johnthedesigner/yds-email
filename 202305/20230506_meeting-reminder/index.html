The Big Spring Meeting: Guest Presentation, Hands On Workshop, Live Auction, Raffle, Tuber/Plant Sale

Sunday, May 7, 2023 from 10:00am to 2:00pm

We’re having our Big Spring Meeting and you won’t want to miss! It’s a wonderful way to kick off the dahlia season and get us all excited for planting time. We’re pulling out all the unicorn tubers from our stash because we want to share the beauty of these cultivars with our members at our 2nd annual tuber auction. In addition we have a wonderful club program lined up with lots of time to socialize and chat dahlias.

From planting to harvest, the tools we use are an indispensable part of gardening. Having the right tools, and maintaining them in good condition makes gardening more fun and easier. We’re having a two part presentation on tools and tool care: In Part 1, YDS Business member Jess Wilke of Flowers at 60 Worcester will share a presentation on her favorite tools and tricks and explain why they’ve made her top list of favorites. Bring some of your own favorite tools to share with us, as gardeners are always looking for something new to add to their tool collection. Be sure to bring along some of your tools that need sharpening and care too, as in Part 2 John Livornese will lead a hands on workshop showing us how and why to sharpen, file and maintain our cutting and digging tools. You will learn what you need and how to sharpen your tools for best performance.

Following the presentations we will have our 2nd annual live dahlia tuber/plant auction and raffle. We have fantastic cultivars that will be offered. Our live auction last year was a blast! Don’t miss the chance to join the fun and come away with some highly desirable and hard to get dahlia plants/tubers!

YDS 2nd Annual Bloom Exhibit - Assistance & Planning Team: We will be in need of club members to volunteer to help with the planning and execution of the weekend's events (Sept. 30th & Oct. 1st).
