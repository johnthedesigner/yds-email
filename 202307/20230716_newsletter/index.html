Are you ready for next Sunday's Club BBQ?

Sunday, July 23, 2023 from 10:00am to 2:00pm

RSVP: https://www.signupgenius.com/go/2023YDSClubBBQ

YDS is looking forward to having our first club BBQ for members and their family. The summer is a wonderful time to hang out with dahlia friends and chat about the growing season. If you have blooms to show and share please bring them. The kids will enjoy play time, games and cold watermelon wedges. We hope you will join us at the BBQ.

In addition to some fun hanging out with each other during the BBQ, we are less than 75 days away from our first Bloom Exhibit that will be held at Spence Farm this fall (September 30th & October 1st). Holding our summer BBQ at Spence Farm next weekend will allow us to get together for a walkthrough and preparation meeting before the Bloom Exhibit. It is so valuable for us to know if you will be able to help be part of the YDS Bloom Exhibit team and can join us at the BBQ so that we all can be ready for our Fall Bloom Exhibit weekend. During the afternoon, we could use some help making more chicken wire balls for our exhibit containers and our Floral Foam Free Exhibit. Together we can make our Bloom Exhibit a wonderful success.

Our BBQ is going to be a summer feast. YDS will supply the hamburgers & chicken sliders and hot dogs. We hope you can bring your favorite summertime dish to share.

Location: Spence Farm @ Hurld Wyman Elementary School, 41 Wyman Street, Woburn, MA 01801

Date & Time: Sunday, July 23, 2023 from 10:00am to 2:00pm (Rain or Shine)

Food: YDS will supply the hamburgers & chicken sliders and hot dogs. We would love for members to bring their favorite summer time cookout dish (and recipe) to share. Some suggested items:

  • Appetizers: Veggies & Dip, Chips & Salsa
  • Fruit: Watermelon, Grapes, Blueberries, Pineapple
  • Salads: Garden Salad, Pasta, Potato, Antipasto, Coleslaw
  • Sides: Corn, Summer Squash, Baked Beans
  • Other: Gluten Free & Vegetarian Dish (*Please Indicate)
  • Dessert: Cookies, Brownies, Pie, Cupcakes

Remember: To bring your folding chair to enjoy time outside on the lawn while we grill and mingle.

Optional: Bring your significant other and your kids. It’s the perfect time to get to know everyone. There will be games for the kids to enjoy while the adults get to chat dahlias.

RSVP: An RSVP is requested and helpful for us as we make plans and have enough sliders and hot dogs. Please let us know you’re coming and if you are bringing a guest family member and kids.
