2024 YDS Tuber Sale Information

The club has grown a large selection of dahlia cultivars in all the forms and most sizes for the garden, exhibition and cut flowers. All tubers have been grown in New England by Yankee Dahlia Society Members and YDS Growing Partners. We hope you keep YDS in mind when making your tuber purchases for the next growing season. By buying tubers from the club sale you are supporting our local dahlia community. Tuber sales are far and away the biggest and most important source of funding for any club and especially for a still new club that has wonderful programming and projects. Each season we are always on the lookout to score desirable cultivars and new introductions to increase our stock so we can offer our members an outstanding selection of club tubers.

We thank our fabulous Growing Partners, the members who have volunteered their time to help plant, grow and divide tubers and our support team of family members who have lent a hand and donated their expertise to make this happen.

We trust that you have enjoyed the programming and new friendships you’ve made and we look forward to a very exciting third year ahead. We are so grateful for your commitment to the club and for wanting to purchase your dahlias from Yankee Dahlia Society. Thank you for your support!

We are proud to hold our YDS member sale online. We have worked hard, with many dedicated hours behind the scenes, to bring you a beautiful online store and an easy shopping experience. Here are the details for our sale.

Shop Details

Tuber Preview: All YDS Members can log in any time to purchase shop merchandise such as books, growing supplies, tools and accessories, soil and amendments. Members will be able to preview the tuber sale, but tubers won’t be available to purchase until the tuber access dates and times listed above. Pricing will become available once the store opens.

Early Access: YDS Members who have volunteered 6 hours of their time towards cutting back, digging and dividing over the past 12 weeks will have exclusive access for a week to our club sale opening on Sunday, January 8, 2023 at 12 noon.

All Member Access: All YDS Members will have access to our club tuber sale on Sunday, January 15, 2023 at 12 noon.

Password: Access to the sale is for YDS members only. You will need to use the email address you provided at registration and a password provided by YDS. You can reset your password at any time by clicking on the Forgot Password link.
