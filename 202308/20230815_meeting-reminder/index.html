Are you ready for tonight's meeting?

Dahlia Basket Arrangements with Eric & Janet Anderson of Sungate’s Dahlias

Tuesday, August 15, 2023 from 7:00am to 8:30pm EDT

*arrive 15 minutes before to get settled and say hello.

Have you ever attended a judged dahlia show and seen a mass display of dahlia blooms arranged in a basket? A presentation of dahlia blooms in a basket is a stunning sight to see. To know that the dahlia grower has displayed their craft of growing multiple dahlia plants to near perfection and arranged the blooms together in a basket is truly remarkable.

Creating basket arrangements for a friend, or to exhibit in a dahlia show can be fun and easy with tips from our guests Eric and Janet Anderson. The Andersons are experienced dahlia growers and are extremely talented in arranging dahlia baskets. Eric and Janet Anderson of Sungate’s Dahlias have been growing, showing and judging dahlias since the late 1980’s. The Andersons are members of Kitsap County Dahlia Society. They garden and hybridize on a third of an acre on the Olympic Peninsula in Bremerton, WA. Eric Anderson is a Senior ADS Judge and both Eric and Janet are award winning exhibitors and hybridizers. “Thirty-five years later our yard now has over 800 plants, nine varieties listed in the ADS classification book, and several new varieties showing well on the ADS Bench.”

Join us for a Zoom presentation as they share their expertise and enthusiasm for growing dahlias, showing dahlias and techniques in arranging baskets. We will learn how to go about selecting baskets to artfully arrange a basket of dahlia blooms of the same cultivar or a combination of different cultivars with foliage to complement. A presentation not to miss as you will be “Wowed” and inspired to create your very own baskets with all your dahlia blooms this fall.
