Calling all YDS Members!!!!

YDS will hold our 2nd annual Dahlia Exhibit free and open to the public on September 30 - October 1, 2023 at Spence Farm @ Hurld Wyman Elementary School in Woburn MA. We will have on display hundreds of beautiful dahlia blooms and creative floral designs featuring dahlias all grown by YDS members and non-members alike for the public to enjoy.

In addition, we will have free floral design and dahlia growing presentations to inspire, educate and celebrate the dahlia.

About the Bloom Exhibit

A Floral Foam Free Exhibit

YDS has decided to make our Dahlia Exhibit a floral foam free event. Instead of floral foam, we will use chicken wire pillows and metal floral pin frogs to support our blooms in reusable display containers. We request that all floral arrangements are also floral foam free.

Dahlia Bouquet Fundraiser

Do you have extra flowers and foliage to spare for the Bouquet Fundraiser? We will be making bouquets to sell during the bloom exhibit and are asking members who have extra flowers and foliage to drop them off for our bouquet station. Please consider bringing buckets of extra blooms, filler and foliage with you on Saturday morning. If you want to drop off early, we will also take buckets of extra flowers/foliage on Thursday evening and Friday morning in Woburn. Coordinate drop off with Misty. Misty’s Cell (732) 674-2087

Would you like to help us make bouquets to sell? Come and join us on Thursday evening to prepare for the weekend’s activities.

Artistic Arrangement Display

Floral arrangements are enjoyed and loved by the public. We are excited to have a display of your dahlia blooms and arrangements for our 2nd Annual YDS Bloom Exhibit. Whether you are new to floral design or have experience, join in on the fun and bring a design to display and share. We love seeing all the creativity from our members and the public.

YDS Bloom Volunteers

We will be in need of club members to volunteer to help with the execution of the weekend’s events (Sept. 30th & Oct. 1st). There are a variety of activities to help with to make our annual YDS Dahlia Exhibit a success. If you have time to help and can sign up that would be greatly appreciated. Many hands will make it fun and light work. Check out ways to help out on SignUp Genius. Thank you so much for stepping up to help! We can’t do it without you.

Activities we need help with:

  • Setup & Breakdown Help
  • Staging Blooms Assistance
  • Bouquet Fundraiser Sales
  • Membership & Information Table
  • Bloom Greeters
  • Answer Dahlia Growing Questions
