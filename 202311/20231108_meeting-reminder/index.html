Are you ready for Thursday's meeting?

Cover Cropping For Field and Garden

Thursday, November 9, 2023 from 6:00pm to 7:30pm

As the growing season winds down and we anticipate lifting dahlias from our garden it’s the perfect time to think about next year’s garden. What will be happening in our garden between the time we lift the dahlias and we replant again in the spring? (We will surely be busy dividing our clumps…but in the meantime, What is happening in our soil?) Building a healthy, resilient soil system is key to the success of any garden, and cover cropping is an easy and effective way to build and maintain a healthy soil foundation year after year.

Collin Thompson, Farm Operations Manager for the Johnny’s Selected Seeds Research Farm in Albion ME will join us for a highly informative presentation about the benefits of cover cropping and how we can apply this to our own gardens. Collin will share with us the key aspects of building a healthy soil foundation including:

  • Determining your cover cropping goals
  • Selecting crop species
  • Establishing and maintaining your cover crop
  • Timing and methods for cover crop termination

Collin Thompson joined Johnny's as Farm Operations Manager in early 2021, having previously managed farms in Virginia, Michigan, and Iowa. He holds a BS in Environmental Biology and a Master's in Sustainable Food Systems.
