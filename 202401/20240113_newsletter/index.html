3rd Annual YDS New Year Celebration

Save the date for Sunday, January 21, 2024 from 11:00am-3:30pm. We will kick off the new year at The Knights of Columbus in Wilmington, MA with a club party. We are celebrating going into our 4th growing season as a dahlia club and ringing in 2024 with a delicious pot luck luncheon, dahlia themed raffle items to choose from as well as door prizes and our special guest speaker, Caitlin Horgan from Flora Flower Farm. For members that purchase from the Y.D.S. tuber sale in January, this will be your opportunity to come pick up your dahlia tuber order.

Special Presentation

A Year Farming Dahlias: The Flora Farm Story

Photos by Caitlin Horgan

Yankee Dahlia Society is excited to present YDS member Caitlin Horgan of Flora Flower Farm in Nassau NY. Caitlin is a flower farmer, beekeeper and floral designer with a passion for dahlias and a unique style inspired by the natural world. Her lush, garden-style florals are grown organically with a deep respect for the environment and sustainability and her stunning photography captures them beautifully. Preserving the environment and wildlife is at the heart of everything she does. Caitlin draws inspiration from nature and art, and she will share how a recent trip to the major art centers of Europe served to deepen her appreciation for what it means to her to grow and design with flowers.

We will hear too about how she integrates social media, a website and technology into her business, which includes a farmstand, CSA’s, farmers and holiday markets, wholesale blooms and wedding florals. Join us as Caitlin shares with us her journey from a career as a teacher to flower farmer and floral designer, helps us understand more about flower farming as a sustainable business, and takes us on a virtual tour through a year on her flower farm.
