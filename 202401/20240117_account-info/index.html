2024 YDS Tuber Sale Information

The club is growing a large selection of dahlia cultivars in all the forms and most sizes for the garden, exhibition and cut flowers. This year over 1,000 tubers have been planted throughout Massachusetts by Yankee Dahlia Society Members and are being cared for by YDS Growing Partners.

2024 YDS Growing Partners

  • Massachusetts Horticultural Society, The Garden at Elm Bank (Wellesley, MA)
  • Nashoba Valley Winery, Distillery, Brewery & Restaurant (Bolton, MA)
  • Trustees of Reservations, Stevens-Coolidge House & Gardens (North Andover, MA)
  • Wright-Locke Farm (Winchester, MA)
  • Woburn Senior Center (Woburn, MA)

YDS Members volunteering at our Growing Partner gardens

We are proud to hold our YDS member sale online. We hope you keep YDS in mind when making your tuber purchases for the 2024 growing season. Each season we are always on the lookout to score desirable cultivars and new introductions to increase our stock so we can offer our members an outstanding selection of club tubers. Tuber sales are far and away the biggest and most important source of funding for any club and especially for a still new club that has wonderful programming and projects. By buying tubers from the club sale you are supporting our local dahlia community.

Thank you for your support!

– Misty & Carol
