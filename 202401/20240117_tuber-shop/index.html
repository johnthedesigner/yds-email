YDS Tuber Shop Information:

Visit https://yankeedahliasociety.com/shop/ and log in with your email and password to shop our selection of tubers and dahlia books, tools, growing supplies and lots of cool stuff.

If you have any questions or need assistance please email info@yankeedahliasociety.com or call/text Misty at (732) 674-2087.

We have lots of varieties to choose from and you’re sure to find many new dahlias you will love to add to your garden this year. We’ll be adding inventory so check back often to snag new cultivars for your garden.

Password: Access to the sale is for YDS members only. You will need to use the email address you provided at registration. You can reset your password at any time by clicking on the Forgot Password link.

Tuber Availability: Tubers added to your cart are not confirmed until paid for. Keep in mind that if there is low availability on a tuber you may want to check out immediately to secure the variety. It is possible that a tuber you put in your cart won’t be available upon checkout if there is limited stock.

Tuber Inventory: We are proud to offer wonderful cultivars; both our tried and true blooms as well as new and popular varieties. In the spring we will add more tubers to the store as the eyes start to develop. This may include new cultivars and more stock in tubers we sell out of during our January sales. Check back often and look out for announcements. Feel free to ask us if you’re looking for something in particular.

Inspection Upon Receipt: All our tubers are planted with a cultivar label during the growing season to make sure what you are ordering is true to the cultivar name. We have gone through rigorous quality checks to ensure that all tubers sold have an eye and each tuber has also been labeled with the cultivar name. Please check your order and store your tubers upon receipt. Let us know within five days of receipt if there is a question with your order. We are not responsible for any storage or growing conditions that cause your tubers to fail.

Dahlia Virus: During the growing season our club participates in the American Dahlia Society’s Clean Stock Initiative where we take leaf samples from a select number of our growing stock to send out to Washington State University to be tested for virus. In addition we purchased a number of Agdia test strips to use in the field. We inspect and cull plants that test positive or are not thriving. While we strive to minimize virus, YDS does not guarantee that the tubers purchased will be free of virus.

Pick Up or Shipping:

You have a choice to have your order shipped in the spring or pick up at our monthly in person meetings.

The first pick up will be at the club New Year Party on Sunday, January 21 at Knights of Columbus in Wilmington MA from 11:00am-3:30pm. Having your tubers in hand in January will mean those who might want to propagate stock from the tubers this year will be able to start their tubers early. This is an extra perk unique to our club sale and buying from YDS.

Not able to attend the January 21st party and meeting, you can pick up at a future club meeting (March 3rd or May 5th at Spence Farm in Woburn, MA), have your order shipped in the spring or coordinate a pick up with Misty or Carol outside of the club meetings. Let us know via SignUp Genius what meeting you would like to pick up your order or if you would like us to ship your order to you in the spring.
