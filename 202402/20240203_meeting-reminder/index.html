Are you ready for tonight's meeting?

Start Your Dahlia Garden with a Plan and Tips from Susan Keyes

Sunday, February 4, 2024 from 4:00pm to 5:30pm EDT

*arrive 15 minutes before to get settled and say hello.

Photo by Brad Keyes

With the days getting longer and spring fast approaching, it won’t be long before the weather will be warm enough to plant the tubers we have purchased or stored over winter. Have you given some thought as to how you might like to group your cultivars or what to plant where? Should you plant en masse, or intermix your dahlias with annuals or perennials? What will be the end result visually once the dahlias bloom? Now is a great time to create our dahlia garden master planting plan! Laying out your garden ahead of time on paper or digitally is invaluable to plan out your garden beds and help you achieve the best result for your unique space.

Gardener and YDS member Susan Keyes grows a profusion of beautiful dahlias in 11 raised beds in Wayland MA and Harpswell ME, with plans for more. Using her camera to record beautiful cultivar combinations and companion plantings she has seen in her own garden and elsewhere, Susan has found her answer to planning her dahlia garden from year to year. She will share with us her method of digitally creating a garden planting plan and planning her cultivar pairings each year through the many photographs she has captured of past dahlia combinations that are truly remarkable. Join us and learn how easy it is to create your own dahlia master garden plan for a uniquely beautiful and successful garden!
